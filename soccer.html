My history with soccer

Since a little kid, I have always been a soccer enthusiast. I grew up watching Messi play and he definitely made an impact in my life. I consider him to be the GOAT of soccer, and that led me to be a die hard FC barcelona fan. As a kid, I used to play striker and scored so many goals, my mom always tells me I was like a little Messi. Nowadays, I like playing more in the midfield, like one of my favorite players, Pedri. Most of my love for soccer comes from watching it. I love watching Champions League as well as the World Cup, which I'm really looking forward to.

My favorite teams

Like I mentioned earlier, I am a die hard Barcelona fan, and that will be a fact about me until I die. However, I like other teams, and sometimes I fluctuate in the teams I like. When it comes to other teams, I like Manchester United, which I started liking when I watched Marcus Rashford play. I also like Juventus because of their amazing defense in the past, but nowadays they are very bad. From my homecountry I am a fan of Saprissa FC. I don't remember why I started liking them but I am pretty sure it has to do with their mascot which is a purple dragon. Another European team I like is Paris Saint-Germain, but only because I traveled to France and bought a shirt there.

My favorite soccer players

Like any good soccer fan, of course I have a list of my favorite soccer players. Throughout time, I have been able to see about 2 or 3 generations of soccer players go by, and I have my favorites from each era. Also, some players I didn't get to see live but I like are Ronaldinho, Maradona, and Ronaldo Nazario. For me, that is peak soccer.

My top 5 favorite soccer players list is:
  1. Lionel Messi
  2. Marcus Rashford
  3. Pedri Gonzalez
  4. Lamine Yamal
  5. Ronaldinho
This list is based on who I have enjoyed the most, not who I think are the best players ever.
