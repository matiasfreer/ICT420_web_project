My gaming history

I started playing videogames when I was years old and I got my first console, a PS4. Back then I played so much every day, I was addicted to games like Destiny, Black Ops 3, or Minecraft.
As I grew older, I played different games like Fortnite, Warzone, and Fall guys. But in 2021 a big change came, I bought my first Gaming Computer and I built it from scratch.
On my computer I have played countless games, some examples are:

  • Golf it!
  • Geometry Dash
  • Roblox
  • FIFA
  • The Crew 2
  • The Forest & Sons of The Forest
Ultimately, I can say gaming has been a big part of my life, even though with time I have stopped playing as much. My favorite game will forever be Destiny and Destiny 2, I enjoyed them so much for more than 7 amazing years.
